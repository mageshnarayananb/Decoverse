About Us

In a world where digital presence defines success, many local furniture shops remain hidden gems — full of beautifully crafted pieces yet lost in the noise of big online retailers. That’s where Decoverse comes in. Born from the insight that countless furniture makers lack a digital platform, Decoverse partners with local shops to curate their best pieces, shoot stunning visuals, and list them on exclusive online storefronts. We manage everything from cataloging to customer communication, allowing artisans to focus on what they do best — crafting furniture. Our nominal service fee keeps it accessible, even for small-scale vendors. We’re currently building this ecosystem in one city, with plans to expand across India — from Vizag to Mumbai — by collaborating with local dealers for delivery, service, and support. This ensures customers enjoy both variety and local convenience. Decoverse is also developing its own exclusive furniture line. Our in-house design team creates blueprints that are executed by trusted local manufacturers, enabling faster production and localized delivery. Whether you’re a vendor without a digital outlet or a customer looking for authentic, affordable furniture with soul and style — Decoverse is your gateway to the smarter furniture future.
