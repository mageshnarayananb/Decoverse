About Us

In a world where digital presence defines success, many local furniture shops still remain hidden gems — full of beautifully crafted pieces but lost in the noise of big online retailers. That’s where Decoverse steps in. Decoverse was born from a simple insight: countless furniture makers across cities have incredible products but no digital platform to showcase them. We decided to change that. We partner with local shops, curate their best pieces, shoot stunning visuals, and list them on our exclusive digital platforms. From cataloging to customer communication, we manage the entire online front — letting artisans focus on what they do best: crafting furniture. For this service, we charge a nominal fee, making it accessible even for small-scale vendors. Currently, we’re focused on building this ecosystem in one city — but we’re laying the groundwork to scale across India. From Vizag to Mumbai, our model allows us to collaborate with local dealers for delivery, service, and support, ensuring customers enjoy both variety and local convenience. And that’s not all. Decoverse is also designing its own line of exclusive furniture. We conceptualize the designs in-house and share blueprints with trusted partner manufacturers in each city. This means faster production, localized delivery, and a growing network of design-led furniture — the Decoverse way. Whether you’re a vendor without a digital outlet, or a customer looking for authentic, affordable furniture with a local soul and a modern spirit — Decoverse is your gateway to a smarter furniture future.
